DASHAVATAR : Dashavatar ia a popular form of theatre with a history of eight hundred years. The term Dashavatar refers to the ten incarnation of lord Vishnu the hindu God of preservation. The ten incarnations are Matsya(fish), Kurma(tortoise), Varaha(boar), Narasimha(lion man), Vaman(dwarf), Parashuram,Ram, Krishna , Buddha and Kalki. It is performed during the annual festival of the village deity in the temple premises after midnight.

HOLI : People in this region celebrate holi in different fashion. Here holi is celebrated for 9 days on the day of holi Purnima.15 to 20 feet tall trunk of biba tree is placed straight and decorated with mango leaves and left to burn for nine days. Last night of holi has folk art ie. Dashavatar Play and oli ends with Dhulivandan.
VEGITATION
Gele is cozily located in the southern ranges of Sahyadri hills. the climate conditions gives rise to a wide range of habitats. Tree likes mangoes, Jackfruit, Blueberry, Kindal. The Habitat ranges from the tropical semi-evergreen forests. Tropical moist deciduous forest subtropical broad leaf hill forest, tropical dry deciduous forests, tropical thorn vegetation, grasslands and agriculture lands.

WILD LIFE : Tiger, Lepord, Wildboar ,Indian Bison ,Deer, Fox, Wolf, Porcupine, Rabbit & Different species of Lizards ,Birds &Snakes are found here.

AGRICULTURE : Rice & Sugar Cane are mainly cultivated in this region. Ragi is also cultivated in some parts earlier Sunflower was also cultivated but later stopped as it affects the growth of Neighbour crops and damages the soil.

Vernacular Architecture : Previously the houses were made of Mud ,Reed & Straws but due to heavy rainfull these houses use to get damage. So ,the mud house were replaced by laterite stone which is locally available & mangler tile for roofing.

Building material : Laterite stone, bricks, cement roofing sheets G.I sheets, mangler tiles are mainly as building material in the region.

Tourism : This place is a hill station having many ironic places to visit. The Greeney deep valleys ,rivers, wildlife fascinate people to visit this place, but the tourism is yet not well developed in this place due to lack o f accommodation facilities for people ,etc.
